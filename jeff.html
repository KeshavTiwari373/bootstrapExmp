Jeff Bezos Profile

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos (born January 12, 1964, Albuquerque, New Mexico, U.S.) American entrepreneur who played a key role in the growth of e-commerce as the founder and chief executive officer of Amazon.com, Inc., an online merchant of books and later of a wide variety of products.Under his guidance, Amazon became the largest retailer on the World Wide Web.
