John Abraham Profile

John Abraham

John Abraham (born 17 December 1972) is an Indian actor, model, writer, and film producer who works in Hindi films. Known for his stoic action hero persona, he is a recipient of a National Film Award along with nominations for four Filmfare Awards. Abraham has appeared in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2017 based on his income and popularity.
